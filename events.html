Pet First Aid - NSPCA Adult Education (In Person @ NSPCA Dublin)

Sunday, March 29 from 10 am to 3 pm

This is a one day workshop for adults in person at the DSPCA in Rathfarnham.

When your pet needs you most, would you know what to do?

Pets are family. Just like us, they can have accidents, sudden illnesses, or emergencies when we least expect them. In those critical moments, your actions can make a real difference. Pet first aid doesn't replace veterinary care but it can save precious time. It helps you recognise the signs of an emergency, stay calm under pressure, comfort your pet, and reduce the risk of their condition worsening while you're getting professional help. This course empowers you with the confidence and practical skills to act when every second counts. You'll learn how to respond safely and effectively to common pet emergencies, giving your pet the best possible chance until they can be seen by a vet. Learning pet first aid is designed to be engaging, hands-on, and enjoyable, while still delivering vital, potentially life-saving knowledge. Whether you're a devoted pet owner or work professionally with animals, this course equips you with skills you'll hopefully never need but will be endlessly grateful to have. For more information...
