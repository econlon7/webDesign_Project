  2. Dublin
    1. Dog Angels
      1. Dogs Angels Ireland is a volunteer-run dog rescue charity based in Dublin, with dedicated volunteers, supporters and fosterers working across the country. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs who, through no fault of their own, have found themselves abandoned, neglected, or unwanted. For more information here are links to the Website, Facebook and Instagram
    2. Dogs Aid Animal anctuary
      1. Dogs Aid is a Dublin based rescue that has been running since 1987. We help to rescue, rehabilitate & rehome unwanted and abandoned dogs. For more information here are links to the Website, Facebook and Instagram
    3. Dogs Trust (ROI)
      1. Dogs Trust is Ireland's largest dog welfare charity. Since establishing our rehoming centre in November 2009, together with our supporters, we have rehomed over 21,000 dogs in Ireland. Working with local authority pounds and animal welfare charities across Ireland, we take in abandoned and neglected dogs who, through no fault of their own, need help getting back on their paws. For more information here are links to the Website, Facebook, Instagram, X,Tiktok and Youtube
    4. Dublin County Council Dog Shelter
      1. Dublin County Dog Shelter is dedicated to the care, welfare, and rehoming of stray and surrendered dogs in Co. Dublin. We provide a safe haven, medical support, and work to find loving forever homes for every dog in our care. For more information here are links to the Facebook and Instagram
    5. Dublin Dog Hub
      1. Currently under contract to Dublin City Council's Animal Welfare Unit to provide safe, secure and comfortable accommodation to stray, seized or unwanted dogs. For more information here are links to the Website and Facebook
    6. Rehoming Irish GreyHound Team (RIGHT)
      1. RIGHT is a greyhound rescue dedicated to finding loving homes for retired Irish greyhounds in Ireland. For more information here are links to the Website, Facebook and Instagram
    7. Working Animal Guardians - WAG Rescue
      1. We are a small, foster based dog rescue operating in Dublin and Meath. For more information here are links to the Facebook and Instagram
  3. Kilkenny
    1. A Dog's Life
      1. A Dog's Life is a registered Irish charity which highlights the plight of dogs in danger of being euthanised in Irish pounds, and promotes the rehoming of pound dogs. We currently mainly take dogs from Waterford City & County dog pound. Because we have no premises of our own, unless we have a suitable adoptive or foster home, we cannot take a dog out of the pound into our care. We are heavily dependent on donations from the public to help dogs, especially with medical bills, as many dogs arrive at the pound in a poor state. Click for more info
    2. Carlow Kilkenny Dog shelter
      1. The shelter is jointly operated by both Carlow and Kilkenny County Councils and is located at Garryduff, Paulstown, Co Kilkenny. R95 WK68. For more information here are links to the Facebook and Website
  4. Laois
    1. Cara Rescue Dogs
      1. Cara Rescue Dogs is a voluntary community group with charitable status. Formed in 2011 by a small group of people in Co. Laois with a shared love of animals. We rescue dogs and place them in a loving foster home until we can find them a forever home. For more information here are links to the Website, Facebook and Instagram
    2. Husky Rescue Ireland
      1. Husky Rescue Ireland (HRI) was set up to alleviate the suffering caused to huskies by irresponsible ownership, abuse and neglect in Ireland. Due to the increase in akitas needing homes recently and lack of rescue space, we also dedicate our time rehoming them. HRI also aims to reduce the social impact of abandoned and un-neutered huskies and akitas in communities around Ireland. For more information here are links to the Website, Facebook, Instagram and Tiktok
