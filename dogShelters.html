Local dog shelters

Leinster

  • Kilkenny
    • A Dogs Life
      • A Dog’s Life is a registered Irish charity which highlights the plight of dogs in danger of being euthanised in Irish pounds, and promotes the rehoming of pound dogs. We currently mainly take dogs from Waterford City & County dog pound. Because we have no premises of our own, unless we have a suitable adoptive or foster home, we cannot take a dog out of the pound into our care. We are heavily dependent on donations from the public to help dogs, especially with medical bills, as many dogs arrive at the pound in a poor state. Click for more info
    • Carlow Kilkenny Dog shelter
      • The shelter is jointly operated by both Carlow and Kilkenny County Councils and is located at Garryduff, Paulstown, Co Kilkenny. R95 WK68. For more information here are links to the Facebook Page and Website
  • Carlow
    • Protectng Pound Dogs Rescue
      • A VOLUNTARY ANIMAL RESCUE REGISTERED CHARITY 20204481 that rehome's Dogs mainly from the Pound or are unwanted and rehabilitate, complete vet work and rehome them to caring responsible homes. link to Facebook
  • Laois
    • Cara Rescue Dogs
      • Cara Rescue Dogs is a voluntary community group with charitable status. Formed in 2011 by a small group of people in Co. Laois with a shared love of animals. We rescue dogs and place them in a loving foster home until we can find them a forever home. For more information here are links to the Website, Facebook and Instagram
